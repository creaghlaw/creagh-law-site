FAQ

Common questions.

When are you available?

Evenings and weekends — that's the model. Most calls happen between 6–9pm on weekdays or during the day on Saturdays. If you need same-week turnaround, just say so on first contact.

How is pricing handled?

Reasonable hourly rates for most matters, with flat fees offered when the scope is clear up front and both of us benefit from the certainty. After the first call, you'll get the rate or quote in writing before we start.

Do you handle litigation?

No. This is transactional and advisory work only. If your matter needs court representation, we'll point you to counsel who can help.

Is the first call free?

Yes — a short intro call so we can both decide if we're a fit. No invoice, no obligation.
