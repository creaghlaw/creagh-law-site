FAQ

Common questions.

When are you available?

Evenings and weekends — that's the model. Most calls happen between 7pm–10pm on weekdays or at a mutually convenient time over the weekend.

How does pricing work?

Most matters are billed at an hourly rate, with flat fees when the scope is clear up front — and either way, you'll get the number in writing before any work begins. Rates stay affordable because the practice runs lean: heavy automation on the admin side keeps overhead low, and there's no expensive office to pay for. Those savings go to you, while the legal work itself stays hands-on and personal.

Do you handle litigation?

No. This is transactional and advisory work only. If your matter needs court representation, we'll happily connect you with counsel who can help.

Where do we meet?

Wherever's convenient for you. A lot can be handled by phone, email, or video, so we only meet in person when it's needed or beneficial — usually for signings. No big boardroom, but everyone gets a good pen to sign with.

Is the first call free?

Yes — a short intro call or email exchange so we can both decide if we're a fit. No invoice, no obligation.
